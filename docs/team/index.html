specialty

  • David's mission is to prevent his clients requiring Spinal Surgery via a Comprehensive Assessment, Diagnose and STATE OF THE ART TREATMENT been, Non-Surgical Spinal Decompression FOR LOW BACK AND NECK PAIN.

Robert Cuffe
Chartered Physiotherapist

Robert Cuffe is a chartered physiotherapist with a keen interest in sport, strength and conditioning, manual therapy, non-surgical spinal decompression therapy and helping his patients reach their full potential.

Specialty

  • Robert's experience has seen him gain knowledge in assessing, diagnosing and treating a wide range of both chronic and acute injuries as well as treating low back and neck pain through non-surgical spinal decompression therapy.
  • Robert continues to compete as an athlete with Kickboxing Ireland and is a multiple time national champion also winning a WAKO world silver medal in 2017. His other interests include soccer, golf and rugby.
  • Using his experience as both an athlete and a physiotherapist, Robert aims to work with his patients in order to optimise their function and enhance their performance, helping them both move, feel and perform to the best of their abilities.
