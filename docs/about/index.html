What is Sports Physiotherapy?

Sports Physiotherapy is a physiotherapist who specialises in treating injuries and issues related to sports people.

Sports injuries can differ to non-sporting injuries as these types of injuries are normally associate with trauma.

At DC Physiotherapy Clondalkin Lucan Dublin 22 we are experts in treating Sports Injuries.

Our Physiotherapist's are trained to use sophisticated manual muscle testing. This enable us to assess a high level athlete's dysfunctional movement patterns. Once the dysfunctional movement patterns have been identified. we can prescribe exercise to address the cause of pain or dysfunction and restore the athlete to their full potential.

DC Physio Clondalkin aim to get our clients out of pain asap so they can perform at their very best.
